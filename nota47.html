The Portrait of Dorian Gray: realización de autorretratos

Por Mariana Palacios, Profesora de Inglés

class="irgaleria" VER MÁS IMAGENES

Today both, 4th years A and B, finished our project "THE PORTRAIT OF DORIAN GRAY" AND OUR PORTRAITS. We've been reading this wonderful story by Oscar Wilde since April!
We wanted to experience the process of making a portrait of our classmates, to see how much of our feelings and souls we may put in the pictures we painted.
Because a portrait is not only the image of the sitter, it also reveals the feelings of the artist.
